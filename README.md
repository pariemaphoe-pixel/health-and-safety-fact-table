# health-and-safety-fact-table

This projects focus on designing a Health and Safety data model that transforms raw dincidents data into meaningful bussiness insights.

At the core of the model is afact table for incidents,connected to key dimensions such as date,employee,location,incident type,severity.and department.It also incorporates critical impact measures like lost time days and medical costs-enabling a deeper understanding of both operational and financial consequences.
The goal of this model is not just to store data,but to ensure that bussiness objectives are clarly answered through structured,reliable and analysis-ready datasets.

With the model organisations can
-Monitor incident trends over time
-Identify high-risk locations and departments
-Evaluate the true cost of workplace incidents
-Support proactive decision making in health and safety managment.

Well structured data is the foundations of effective decision making
